MEET THE DEMONS

Kokushibo (Upper Moon One)

A powerful demon who was once a Demon Slayer and the twin brother of Yoriichi, using Moon Breathing with terrifying swordsmanship.

Doma (Upper Moon Two)

A charming yet cruel demon who leads a cult and uses deadly ice-based techniques to freeze his enemies.

Akaza(Upper Moon Three)

A martial artist demon who respects strength and despises the weak, using powerful hand-to-hand combat

Hantengu (Upper Moon Four)

A cowardly demon who splits into multiple powerful clones representing different emotions like anger and sorrow.

Gyokko (Upper Moon Five)

A grotesque and vain demon with a pot-like body, who uses vases and summons deadly aquatic creatures.

Daki and Gyutaro (Previous Upper Moon Six)

Sibling demons who fight together, with Daki using her obi sashes and Gyutaro wielding blood scythes.
